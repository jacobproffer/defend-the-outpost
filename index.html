Defend the Outpost

An Arma 3 scenario

The SITREP

The quarantine of Altis has failed and the few remaining survivors must band together at a remote outpost near Sagonisi.

You and three other unlucky souls must do everything possible to survive the onslaught of infected in this Arma 3 scenario.

Gathered intelligence

It's up to you and three comrades to defend the last outpost of Altis against a horde of over a hundred infected. Ammo is limited but an armed Greyhawk is on standby to provide close air support. Can you make it out alive?
